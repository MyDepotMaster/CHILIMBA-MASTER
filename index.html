How it works

Set it up once. It runs every cycle after that.

01

Set up your group

Contribution amount, cycle length, and rotation order — entered once when the group starts.

02

Log each payment

Cash or mobile money, per member, per cycle. Outstanding balances are flagged automatically.

03

Chilimba Master tells you who's next

The rotation is tracked for you, so there's no more relying on memory — or arguing about it.

04

Share the summary

One tap builds a "who's paid, who owes" message, ready to paste straight into your group's WhatsApp.

What's inside

Everything a treasurer actually needs

Rotation engine

Contribution tracking

Log payments per member, per cycle — cash or mobile money, with late flags.

Ledger & balance

Total collected, total paid out, and the pot balance — always current.

WhatsApp summaries

Printable statements

Loans & penalties

For groups that lend from the pot — interest, due dates, and late fines, automated.
